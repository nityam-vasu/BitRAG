# THE UNKNOWN - Character Profiles & Backstories (Indian Context)

---

## DR. AISHA SHARMA - The Protagonist

### Background

Dr. Aisha Sharma stands at thirty-two as one of the most promising psychiatrists in Bombay, though her gender has made every achievement an uphill battle. She graduated from Grant Medical College in 1919, a time when women in medicine were still viewed as novelties rather than professionals. Her dissertation on traumatic memory was revolutionary for its era, arguing that patients' unexplained physical symptoms often stemmed from suppressed psychological trauma.

But Aisha's interest in the mind goes beyond academic curiosity. At twelve years old, she witnessed her mother, Dr. Sunita Sharma, walk into the mist-shrouded garden of their Bombay home and simply vanish. No footprints, no trace, no explanation. The police called it a runaway case; Aisha knew better. Her mother would never have left without her locket—the silver piece Aisha still wears around her neck to this day.

### The Locket

The locket is Aisha's most treasured possession and her greatest mystery. Inside is a faded photograph of her mother, but the image is strange—half of Sunita's face is clear, the other half seems to shimmer.

---

## THOMAS RAVANHALLI - The Patriarch

### Background

At fifty-eight, Thomas Ravanhalli carries the weight of three generations of family secrets on his shoulders. He inherited the Ravanhalli estate at twenty-three when his father suffered a mental collapse. Thomas has maintained the family's crumbling legacy, keeping the dark truths buried beneath polite Victorian propriety.

### His Role in the Mystery

Thomas knows more about The Watcher than he will ever admit. He has seen it—glimpsed it in mirrors, felt its presence. When Marcus died, Thomas saw an opportunity to end the cycle.

### The Secret He Keeps

Aisha is not simply Marcus's niece. In 1894, when Sunita arrived at Ravanhalli Manor, pregnant and fleeing something she couldn't name, a ritual was performed—the one that would give the child a connection to The Watcher.

---

## ADITI RAVANHALLI - The Daughter

### Background

At twenty-six, Aditi Ravanhalli has lived her entire life in the shadow of Ravanhalli Manor. Her mother died giving birth to her—a fact Thomas never lets Aditi forget. She grew up believing she was cursed.

### Her Connection to The Watcher

Aditi has seen The Watcher since childhood. She learned early not to speak of it; her father's reaction to her childhood drawings taught her that some truths are too dangerous to voice.

---

## DR. MARCUS RAVANHALLI - The Deceased

### Background

Dr. Marcus Ravanhalli (1852-1924) was the black sheep of the family—a brilliant scholar who pursued forbidden knowledge with obsessive dedication. He studied at Cambridge, specializing in occult anthropology.

### His Research

Marcus spent decades documenting the family's history. By 1920, he believed he had finally understood The Watcher—and how to communicate with it.

### His Death

Marcus died in the manor's library, a room he had sealed. The door was locked from the inside; his body showed no signs of violence.

---

## CAPTAIN HARI PRAKASH - The Witness

### Background

At sixty-seven, Hari Prakash has spent fifty years trying to forget what he saw in September 1894. As a young man of seventeen, he was the sole survivor of the event that claimed seven lives—the Ravanhalli Festival.

### What He Knows

Hari is the only living person who can provide firsthand testimony about 1894. He knows that Sunita was involved in the ritual that preceded the disappearances.

---

## DR. MEERADESAI - The Rival

### Background

Dr. Meera Desai is everything Aisha aspires to be—and everything she fears becoming. A prominent psychiatrist at Breach Candy Hospital, Meera has built her career on dismissing "feminine weakness."

### Her Secret Agenda

Meera came to Kodalipur for the same reason as Aisha—to investigate Marcus's research—but for entirely different purposes. She believes understanding The Watcher could revolutionize psychiatry.

---

## THE WATCHER - The Entity

### Nature

The Watcher defies simple categorization. It is not a ghost or spirit—it is something that exists outside human understanding. It perceives reality not as humans do, but as a continuous flow of states.

### The Truth About Aisha

Aisha is the final product of the original bargain. Born during the ritual of 1894, she was conceived specifically to be the perfect bridge—a consciousness that could exist partially in The Watcher's realm. Her mother's disappearance was not a tragedy but a translation: Sunita chose to become part of The Watcher.

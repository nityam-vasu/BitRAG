# THE UNKNOWN - Chapter Six (Indian Context)

## The Morning After

The sun rose over Kodalipur for the first time in decades. Aisha watched from the window.

"You're leaving," Thomas said.

"I have to. There are patients in Bombay who need me."

"But before I go—I need to understand what happens next."

---

## Aditya's New Role

They found her in the chamber. "The between is part of me now," Aditya said. "The Watcher is gone—not destroyed, just at peace."

"What will you do now?"

"Stay. Protect the door. Train others who come after me."

---

## Holloway's Fate

Meera Desai lay in bed, her eyes distant. "I remember trying to... to control something vast."

"The entity you tried to control has been released."

"Released?" Her eyes sharpened with fear. "You let it go?"

"You tried to control something that couldn't be controlled."

---

## Captain Hari's Farewell

The old fisherman waited on the front steps. "I've been waiting for fifty years to see that mist lift."

"How do you know they're at peace?"

"Because you told Aditya. And Aditya told me. And I believe her."

---

## The Reconciliation

Meera sought Aisha out. "I want to help. Not to control. To understand."

Aisha considered. "Alright. You can help."
